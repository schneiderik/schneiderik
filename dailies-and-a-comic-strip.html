September 2011 Daily Comics & "A Comic Strip"

It's always been a dream of mine to have a daily comic strip, but as I've found, it's a much larger undertaken than I assume. I usually make it only a month before giving up. It's most likely due to the fact that I over estimate the time I will have to work on comics. I make the art too time consuming, fall behind my daily schedule, and move on to other projects. At the end of this try, I drew a comic about the attempt, as well as some more personal health problems I was going through at the time.
