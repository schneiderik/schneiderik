Sand Dune collects six minicomics into a small perfect-bound book. It includes Erik & Ashley, Quiet Fishing Village, Whatever Happens, Autobiographic Fanfiction, Nice Tat and Toucher.

The name comes from when I first started making comics. I originally published my minis under the title Sand Dune No. 1, Sand Dune No. 2, etc. with the comic title secondary on the inside of the book. I ended up abandoning this idea and reprinted most of my minis without the Sand Dune moniker. For this collection I decided to bring it back, and create a final resting place for these stories to live.
