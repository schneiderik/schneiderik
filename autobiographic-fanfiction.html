Autobiographic Fanfiction

In anticipation of exhibiting at CAKE 2012, I put together three small 8-page mini-comics, each of which were works of fiction about my past, present, and future respectively. For each cover, I took a headshot of myself and doodled on it. I thought they were great; however sadly, it seems I've thrown away all of the originals, deleted the photoshop files and (less sadly) sold out of all of the copies.
