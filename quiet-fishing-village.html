Quiet Fishing Village

Growing up in the heart of West Michigan, or "The Bible Belt of the High Five", religion has always played a large role in my life. It was a slow journey to the place I am now. Education about other cultures and beliefs painted a picture for me that made religion seem all the more cult-like. Muskegon, Michigan hasn't been known for its commercial fishing in any time period, but a small fishing village setting seemed more fitting for a campy cult-triller than Quiet Logging Town.
