Keep Off Dunes

This is my 2012 contribution to a free comic book day minicomic titled Handout Comics that Sam Sharpe and Neil Brideau have put together and edited the last three years. A lot of other Chicago artists contributed to the 2012 issue, including Jeremy Sorese, Bernie McGovern, Jeremy Tinder, Caroline Picard, Ian McDuffie, Nate Beaty, Beth Hetland, and Jeff Zwirek.
