Thirty Days of Comics 2013

It had been a long time since I made comics. So I wanted to ease myself back into the routine. Coincidentally, November was just around the corner, and I saw on twitter that a bunch of people do Thirty Days of Comics over the course of the month. It was the perfect opportunity. Looking back on the attempt, I should have made the comics less time consuming. Also, I should have looked ahead, because when I started the month I wasn't thinking about the fact that I would be leaving for Paris on the 23rd.

I successfully kept up with it for 22 days, but didn't commit to drawing overseas. The comics from Paris were drawn over the following month. I spent much more time on them as it was my first time out of North America and a very special experience.
