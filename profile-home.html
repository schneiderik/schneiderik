Profile Home

I have started so many projects that have eventually fallen through. I'm always so sure of myself in the beginning that I cut bristol board for the whole story and then never end up doing it. I've done this so many times that Ashley challenged me to stop buying new paper until I had used up all of my pre-cut pieces. Profile Home is a collection of small illustrations drawn on the more oddly sized pieces.
